## Overview
This project is a Google Sheets-based analytics and reporting system designed to manage influencer marketing campaigns from outreach to performance tracking. It combines CRM workflow management, lead tracking, ROI analysis, and reporting into a structured operational system.

The goal was to improve campaign visibility, streamline tracking, and support data-driven decision-making through dashboards and reporting workflows.

---

## Features

- Influencer outreach and campaign tracking
- Lead attribution and conversion tracking
- KPI dashboards and campaign reporting
- ROI and performance analysis
- Weekly reporting summaries
- Decision memos and action tracking
- Workflow visibility and follow-up management

---

## Tools Used

- Google Sheets
- Advanced Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Dashboarding & Charts
- Conditional Formatting
- KPI Reporting Frameworks

---

## Business Problems Addressed

- Unstructured campaign workflows
- Missed follow-ups and low visibility
- Difficulty tracking campaign performance
- Lack of centralized reporting
- Inconsistent operational monitoring

- Improved reporting visibility through structured dashboards and summaries
